Individual Award

To qualify for an individual nomination, candidates must meet the following requirements:

  1. Must be a member of staff who has worked continuously for a period of one year.
  2. Has demonstrated professional excellence in service delivery.
  3. Serving in any cadre within the school at the time of nomination.
  4. Maintained excellent performance appraisal score.
  5. Is not subject of an ongoing discipline case.


Campus Employee of the Year

THIS NOMINATION WILL BE AVAILABLE AT QUARTER FOUR

Nominee Type:This category is open for individual nominations.

Award Description: This award recognizes an individual who has promoted and upheld integrity as provided in chapter six of the Constitution, values and principles in Article 232 and National Values in Article 10 and KSG Values. The officer must also have made a significant impact on service delivery in the provision of efficient, effective and quality services.

  1. Must have been an employee of the nominating campus for one continuous year in the year of nomination.
  2. Demonstrated the criteria defined in part 4:
    • Outstanding Customer Service
    • Creativity and Innovation
    • Teamwork
    • Leadership Abilities
    • Empathy and Support for others
    • Community Involvement
  3. Demonstrated improved access to, as well as the delivery of service in the campus
  4. Positively influenced change in colleague’s perception while improving transparency in delivery of service.

*Nominees's name:

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Outstanding Customer Service?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Creativity and Innovation?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Teamwork?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Leadership Abilities?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Empathy and Support for others?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this person on Community Involvement?

Most Improved Employee Award

Nominee Type:This category is open for individual nominations.

Award Description: The award shall recognize an employee who has demonstrated significant improvement in their Performance through the school performance appraisal system. The scores of the performance appraisal will form the basis of this award. Where there is a tie, the Campus Performance Committee shall seek the supervisor’s recommendations.

Values Champion Award

Nominee Type:This category is open for individual nominations.

Award Description: The award will recognize an individual in the campus who has promoted and upheld integrity as provided in chapter six of the Constitution, values, and principles in Article 232 and National Values in Article 10 and KSG Values.

  1. Must have been an employee of the nominating campus for one continuous year in the year of nomination.
  2. Should be acknowledged by colleagues as a person of integrity whose character is beyond reproach.
  3. Must be a respectable, well groomed person who promotes public confidence in the office.
  4. Must be honest and selfless while delivering service.
  5. An individual that is time conscience and a team player.

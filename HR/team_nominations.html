Team Award

To consist of a team nomination, the team must meet the following qualifications:

  1. Must consist of employees of the school having served for a period of one year.
  2. Demonstrate professional excellence in service delivery as a team.


Team Award

Nominee Type:This category is open for team nominations.

Award Description:A team refers to a group of individuals, units/ departments.

  1. Must fall within the definition of a team as stated above.
  2. Demonstrate the criteria defined in part 4.
  3. Every campus at the school will award this category

*Select one team:

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Outstanding Customer Service?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Creativity and Innovation?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Teamwork?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Leadership Abilities?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Empathy and Support for others?

*On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate this team on Community Involvement?
